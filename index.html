California

California hospitals

California has the strongest hospital charity care law in the country — free care up to 350% FPL, discounts to 600% FPL, and no asset testing. All California hospitals on this list benefit from these state-level protections.

New York

New York hospitals

New York's Indigent Care Pool reimburses hospitals for approved charity care, creating strong incentives to approve applications. State law requires collections to pause while any application is under review.

Illinois

Illinois hospitals

Illinois's Hospital Uninsured Patient Discount Act requires free care under 200% FPL and discounts to 600% FPL at many hospitals — and applies to for-profit facilities, unlike the federal rules.
