Montefiore Health System financial assistance: the complete 2026 guide

Montefiore Health System is the primary safety-net hospital system for the Bronx — one of the lowest-income urban counties in the United States. Montefiore operates 14 hospitals including the flagship Moses Campus, Einstein Campus (Weiler), and Wakefield Campus, and serves a patient population where financial assistance is critically important. This 2026 guide covers Montefiore's charity care program, New York State Indigent Care Pool protections, and how to apply at any Montefiore facility.

How to apply

How to apply for financial assistance at Montefiore Health System

  1. Request an itemized bill first

    Before doing anything else, call Montefiore Patient Financial Services at 1-718-920-5899 and request a fully itemized statement of every charge on your bill. Review it carefully — billing errors are common. Correcting errors before applying means charity care is applied to the correct baseline amount.

  2. Read the Financial Assistance Policy

    Download and read Montefiore Health System's Financial Assistance Policy at montefioreeinstein.org/patient-care/patients-visitors/billing-insurance/aid-policy. Pay particular attention to the income tiers, documentation requirements, and appeal process before starting the application.

  3. Complete the application

    Download the financial assistance application from the same page, or request a paper copy at any Montefiore Health System admissions or billing office. Complete every section — incomplete applications are the most common reason for processing delays and initial denials.

  4. Gather your documentation

    Collect all documents listed in the section below. Make photocopies of everything before submitting. If mailing, use certified mail with return receipt to create a dated delivery record.

  5. Submit and follow up

    Submit your completed application and supporting documents. Follow up by phone after 2–3 weeks to confirm receipt, get a case reference number, and ask about the expected processing timeline.

  6. Appeal if denied

    A denial is not final. Request the specific reason in writing and submit a formal written appeal with additional or corrected documentation. Read our complete guide to appealing a denied charity care application for a step-by-step process.

Documents needed

What Montefiore Health System typically requires for a financial assistance application

  • Montefiore Financial Assistance Application
  • Most recent federal tax return or W-2s
  • Recent pay stubs (last 2–3 months)
  • Government benefit award letters (Medicaid, Social Security, SNAP)
  • Bank statements if self-employed or income is irregular
  • If no income: signed self-attestation letter (alternative documentation accepted)
What's unique about this hospital's policy

Experienced with non-standard and zero-income situations

Montefiore's patient population is among the most economically diverse of any major US hospital system. Its financial counselors have deep experience with non-standard income situations — gig economy workers, seasonal workers, undocumented patients, patients with recent job loss, and those with zero verifiable income. If your income situation is complicated or doesn't fit standard documentation requirements, Montefiore's financial services team is generally well-equipped to work with alternative documentation.
